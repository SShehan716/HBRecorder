1. Introduction

Welcome to Misty Screen Recorder (Lite). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our screen recording application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • App Settings: Your recording preferences, quality settings, and app configurations
  • Storage Access: Permission to save recordings to your device storage

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model and operating system version are read on-device only, to select the right recording configuration for your hardware. This information is never transmitted anywhere.

2.3 Permissions We Request

Our app requires the following permissions to function:

Permission Purpose When Used
RECORD_AUDIO Record system audio during screen recording Only during active recording
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Display floating controls for recording When recording is active
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Keep recording active in background During screen recording
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PROJECTION Capture the screen as a foreground service (Android 14+ requirement) During screen recording
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE Capture microphone audio as a foreground service (Android 14+ requirement) During screen recording, when audio capture is enabled
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Show recording status notifications Android 13+ devices
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Save recordings to device storage Android 9 and below only (not requested on newer Android versions)
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Read your saved recordings for playback, trimming, and the Vault feature Android 12 and below only
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO Read your saved recordings for playback, trimming, and the Vault feature Android 13 and above

Note: This app does not request the INTERNET permission. It cannot and does not connect to the internet or send any data anywhere.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 App Functionality

  • Screen Recording: Process and save your screen recordings to your device
  • Audio Recording: Capture system audio during recordings
  • App Settings: Remember your preferences and configurations
  • Floating Controls: Provide recording controls overlay

3.2 App Reliability

  • Local Error Handling: The app is designed to handle errors and unexpected states gracefully, entirely on your device. We do not receive crash reports or usage data from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs (no Google Play Services Analytics, no Firebase, no AdMob). It has no internet permission and cannot transmit data to us or to any third party.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • Recordings: Stored locally on your device
  • Settings: Saved in app's private storage
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload your recordings to our servers

5.2 Data Security

  • Local Processing: All recording processing happens on your device
  • No Transmission: Recordings are not sent to our servers
  • Secure Storage: App data is stored in Android's secure app storage

5.3 Data Retention

  • Recordings: Stored until you delete them
  • App Data: Retained as long as the app is installed

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Control

6.2 Permission Management

6.3 Opt-Out Options

Not applicable — this app does not collect analytics, advertising, or usage data that would require an opt-out.
